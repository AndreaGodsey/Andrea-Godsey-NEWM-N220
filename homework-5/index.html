Welcome Into the Forest

Friends till the very end...but just how close to your END are you willing to go, dear Player?

You rent a cottage near the edge of a forest, and you are looking for your lost puppy: Snuggles. He ran off after chasing the evil bird that ever dared look at you in such distaste! Unfortunatley, defending your honor came with the price! He is lost, and now you must find him! But be warned, dear Player, for there may be creatures lurking in this small, chilly forest- waiting to find you-Good Luck, brave Player! Find Snuggles-your small yet faithful companion!
